Combinatorics

we calculate combination and permutation here in this page.A combination focuses on the selection of objects without regard to the order in which they are selected. A permutation, in contrast, focuses on the arrangement of objects with regard to the order in which they are arranged.Here we can calculate the combination of m objects taken n at a time.we can give values of m and n ourself and the program calculates the values for permutation or combination.we can choose whether we are going to calculate permutation or combination.We can choose either we are calculating without replacement or with replacement. Permutations of m objects taken n at a time is: p(m,n)=m!/(m-n)!*n! Combinations of n objects taken n at a time is: C(m,n)=m!/(m-n)!
